Gluee is Bladee's first official project, consisting of 9 tracks featuring Thaiboy Digital, ECCO2K and BONES. Produced by Whitearmor, Yung Sherman, Curtis Heron and Blank Body. It was released on January 27, 2014. A music video for the track “Unreal” was released at the same time as the album.
